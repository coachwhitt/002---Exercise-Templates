# Comprehensive Research: Sourcing Anatomical Muscle Diagrams for Exercise Cards

**Research Date:** November 11, 2025
**Project Requirements:** 100+ anatomical diagrams with custom muscle highlighting for commercial fitness use

---

## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

After comprehensive research across stock marketplaces, specialized services, 3D rendering options, AI tools, and freelance platforms, the most cost-effective solutions for 100+ anatomical diagrams are:

1. **Envato Elements** - $198/year for unlimited downloads
2. **WorkoutLabs Train** - $599-$949/year with 3,500+ exercise illustrations built for fitness
3. **AI Generation (Hybrid Approach)** - ~$576 for Midjourney + medical illustrator review
4. **Freepik Premium** - $144/year for commercial use

**Top Recommendation:** WorkoutLabs Train for fitness-specific needs, or Envato Elements for broader design flexibility.
