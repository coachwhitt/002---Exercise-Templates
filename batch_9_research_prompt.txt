======================

For TRICEPS exercises:
- Focus on EMG studies comparing lying vs overhead vs pushdown variations
- Highlight long head activation differences (overhead = maximal long head stretch)
- Document elbow positioning and shoulder angle effects on muscle recruitment
- Include sports requiring triceps strength (throwing, punching, pressing movements)

For NECK exercises:
- Emphasize cervical spine safety and progression protocols
- Reference contact sport injury prevention research
- Document muscle activation: sternocleidomastoid, splenius capitis, trapezius
- Sports focus: American Football, Rugby, Wrestling, MMA, Boxing, Hockey

For ROTATOR CUFF exercises:
- Focus on subscapularis (internal rotation) and infraspinatus/teres minor (external rotation)
- Include shoulder health and injury prevention research
- Document optimal shoulder positioning for isolation
- Sports focus: Baseball, Tennis, Swimming, Volleyball, Overhead Athletes
