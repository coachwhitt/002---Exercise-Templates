**Stability Ball:**

### Data Points to Capture:

1. **Muscle Activation (EMG):**
   - Primary muscles with % MVIC (Maximum Voluntary Isometric Contraction)
   - Secondary muscles with activation patterns
   - Comparison to similar exercises

2. **Biomechanics:**
   - Joint angles and movement patterns
   - Force production and power output
   - Velocity and acceleration data

3. **Training Effects:**
   - Strength gains (% improvement)
   - Power development
   - Hypertrophy outcomes
   - Metabolic demand (VO2, heart rate, lactate)

4. **Injury Prevention:**
   - Common technique errors
   - Landing mechanics (for plyometrics)
   - Joint stress and safety considerations

### Reference Format:
```
Author(s) (Year). Title. Journal Volume(Issue):Pages. DOI or URL
```

**Example:**
```
Schoenfeld BJ et al. (2014). Effects of different volume-equated resistance training loading strategies. J Strength Cond Res 28(10):2909-2918. https://doi.org/10.1519/JSC.0000000000000480
```
